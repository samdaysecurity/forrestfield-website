Support the New Playground

Your donation helps fund the replacement of aging playground equipment at Forrest Field in Ladysmith. This project will provide a safer, more accessible, and more inclusive play space for local families and children.

How to Donate

  1. Send an Interac e‑Transfer to:
    donate@forrestfieldes.ca
  2. Autodeposit is enabled — no password is required.
  3. Complete the form below so we can properly record and acknowledge your donation.

Donations are used directly to support playground replacement and related site improvements at Forrest Field.

Donation Information

Important: The Forrest Field Enhancement Society is a registered society and is not able to issue charitable tax receipts for donations.

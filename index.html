Enhancing Forrest Field for Everyone

A community‑led nonprofit working with the Town of Ladysmith to deliver enhancements at Forrest Field that fall outside the Town’s current budget. Our current priority is replacing aging playground equipment so kids can play safely for years to come.

Our Mission

We support improvements at Forrest Field that promote healthy outdoor activity for people of all ages. The Society formed from a coalition of local service clubs—Ladysmith Rotary, Ladysmith Kinsmen, Ladysmith Eagles, Ladysmith Lions—and Mid Island Soccer, working in conjunction with the Town of Ladysmith to enhance the Lot 108 (Forrest Field) project.

  • Replace aging structures and equipment
  • Improve accessibility and safety
  • Deliver community‑benefit amenities beyond municipal budget

This is a temporary website published to support nonprofit verification. A full website will follow.

Purpose Statement (Summary)

Our purpose is to improve community health and wellbeing by supporting top‑tier outdoor recreation and sporting facilities for all age groups in Ladysmith and central Vancouver Island. Enhancements may include accessible walkways and equipment, fitness trails, BMX park, multi‑use arena, pickleball/tennis courts, and clubhouse improvements.

Current Priority: Playground Replacement

Project Overview

Replace outdated playground structures with modern, safe equipment supplied and installed by a licensed vendor. Following consultation with Habitat Equipment, we have a quote of approximately $136,000 (plus base and site preparation).

  • Scope: Remove and replace aging equipment; improve safety surfacing
  • Features: ~6 new elements including a slide, multiple climbing apparatuses, and a swing set
  • Impact: Safer, more inclusive play for families in Ladysmith

Note: This upgrade is outside the Town’s current budget; community fundraising bridges the gap.

Why It Matters

Forrest Field is a vital community space for healthy outdoor activity. Upgrading the playground ensures longevity, improves safety, and encourages active play for children of all ages and abilities.

Future Phases of Development

In addition to the playground replacement project, the Forrest Field Enhancement Society and the Town of Ladysmith are advancing a long‑term multi‑phase plan for the Lot 108 area. These enhancements are designed to support active lifestyles, community connection, and outdoor recreation for all ages.

  • Perimeter Trail – A looped walking path connecting the recreation area
  • Outdoor Fitness Area – Public workout equipment integrated along the trail
  • Bike Skill Development Area – Dedicated zone for skill building and safe cycling practice
  • Pickleball Courts – Multi‑court outdoor facility
  • Upper & South Parking Areas – Added capacity for events and busy days
  • Practice Pitch – Expanded training field area

These items reflect current concept planning and may evolve as the project advances.

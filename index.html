Our Mission

The Society formed from a coalition of Ladysmith Rotary, Kinsmen, Eagles, Lions, and Mid Island Soccer. We work with the Town of Ladysmith to enhance the Lot 108 / Forrest Field project.

  • Replace aging structures and equipment
  • Improve accessibility and safety
  • Support lifelong outdoor recreation

Purpose Statement (Summary)

Our purpose is to support top‑tier outdoor recreation facilities for all age groups in Ladysmith and central Vancouver Island.

Current Priority: Playground Replacement

Project Overview

New equipment package quoted at $136,000 (plus base/prep) from Habitat Equipment, including ~6 components such as slides, climbing structures, and swings.

Why It Matters

Forrest Field is a key community recreation area. New equipment ensures safety, accessibility, and long‑term value to families.

Future Phases of Development

The Society and the Town of Ladysmith are jointly planning long‑term improvements for the Lot 108 area.

  • Perimeter Trail
  • Outdoor Fitness Area
  • Bike Skill Development Area
  • Pickleball Courts
  • Upper & South Parking Areas
  • Practice Pitch

Final elements may evolve as the project progresses.
